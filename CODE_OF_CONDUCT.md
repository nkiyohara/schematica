# Code Of Conduct

Schematica should stay practical, technical, and respectful.

Participants are expected to:

- discuss ideas and code without personal attacks
- keep issue reports reproducible and on topic
- respect maintainers' decisions about scope, release timing, and distribution
  risk
- avoid sharing private credentials, logs, or personal data unless explicitly
  requested through a safe channel

Harassment, threats, spam, and bad-faith disruption are not acceptable.
Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that do not meet this
standard.
